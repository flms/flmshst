Admin

Admin software, in addition to showing restaurant financial reports, it has the ability to make changes on restaurant's menu and send it on demand to all faralams products to the same local network.

Installable on Windows, Linux and MAC - to be used by restaurant manager

  • Innovative Design

    At a glance you can see sales reports on specific time

  • Operating fast

    you can change restaurant menu on demand and send it to connected devices on local network

  • Managing devices

    Ability to change digital menu's appearance

  • Adding new Languages

    Add up to 9 different languages to your digital menus and let your customers order food by their own language.

  • Quick reports

    At a glance you can see daily, monthly and yearly sales and compare them together.

  • Sending information

    After saving your preferred changes, you can easily send menu to other devices on the connected local network.

  • Fast viewing Customer's Bill

    By using a filter bar, you can see receipt on a specified range of time.

  • Digital menu appearance

    With a few clicks you can apply your preferred graphical changes to the digital menus connected to the network.

  • Receipt List

    After applying filters using filter bar, you can see a list of receipts

  • Printer Selection

    You can select any printer to print receipt using admin software

  • Quick Report

    After applying filters using filter bar and listing your preferred list, you can see a box of financial reports.

  • Accessing to the receipts

    In case of needing to change or showing a receipt content, you can move between registered receipts by different dates

  • Changing printer output

    If you want to print a customer receipt, you can edit receipt and print it
