Digital Menu (Tablet)

After installing this product on your tablets, it would be turned to a digital menu which your waiters can give it to your customers instaed of your old menu

Extraordinary and simple design with support of multiple language bring a memorable experience for you customers. This application is available to all range of tablets on the market

  • Multilingual menu

    By adding more language you would let your international customers to select foods on their own language

  • Instant change on menu

    You would no longer need to print new menu due to price change or new foods and pictures, you can change your menu every time you want

  • Easy to order

    After giving tablet to customers, your customers would order themselves

  • Software Settings

    All management settings are hidden and after entering Software settings you can see all of them

  • Menu update

    In case of needs to get latest changes from server, you can click on update button

  • language selection

    You can change menu language to your customer language before delivering tablets and then receive orders on server on your own language

  • Add / Remove

    Customers can easily select and remove items and see selected items count

  • Categories

    Customers can easily move between different categories
